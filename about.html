About

I spent 10 years advising North Carolina Teachers and State Employees on every aspect of their financial lives. During this time I was saddened to see that many of these middle class people were taken advantage of by financial professionals selling terrible, commission based products. At the end of 2018 I finally decided to do something about it. I walked away from my business that I had spent 10 years building and joined an independent firm that would allow me to charge a fee for financial advice. My goal, to offer affordable fiduciary advice that was not tied to the sale of any product and to help state employees make good financial decisions as they work there way to and through retirement.

I am a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional and a Retirement Income Certified Professional. I’m an active member of the Financial Planning Association and a proud Rotarian. I served as a mentor in Gaston County Schools for 7 years and enjoy coaching my children’s soccer and basketball teams. My family and I are members of Belin United Methodist Church.

In my free time I enjoy running, golfing, fishing and spending time with my family. My daughter Mary-Lois is 10 years old and my son Wilson is 9. Maddie dog turns 8 in January.
