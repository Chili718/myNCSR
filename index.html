Who Am I?

My name is Bob Furlong. I’m a CERTIFIED FINANCIAL PLANNER™ professional who has been helping North Carolina Teachers and State Employees plan for retirement since 2010. My mission is to help you make smart, educated, financial decisions on your journey to and through retirement.

Ideal Clientele

My ideal clients are North Carolina teachers, firefighters, police offers and local government officials who are within 5 years of retirement. They want to devote time to understanding how best to prepare for retirement both emotionally and financially. They’ve saved for retirement along the way and need financial planning and help preparing a retirement income plan.

How to Become an Ideal Client

A personal consultation is a way for me to help state employees become the ideal client. I recognize that affordable, fiduciary advice is out of reach for many state employees. Making smart, educated, financial decisions throughout the different career stages will have a profound impact on your retirement lifestyle. Hire me and rest assured knowing you are getting independent advice that is in your best interest and not tied to the sale of financial services products.

A Deep Understanding No Matter the Stage in your Career

Career Stages

There are 4 stages in a North Carolina State employee’s career. All stages have overlapping circumstances like saving for retirement and living within your means. Each stage will also have very unique circumstances like student loans, getting married, having children, saving for college, and planning for retirement.
